The search problem

  • Search: "authentication"
  • Cards: login, sign-in, OAuth
  • Words differ, meaning overlaps
Keyword search matches words. Semantic search matches meaning.

What is an embedding?

  • Text as numbers
  • Similar meaning → similar vectors
  • Different meaning → distant vectors

Text becomes vectors

"Deploy the API"
→ [0.021, -0.184, 0.553, 0.091, ..., -0.337]

"Ship the backend service"
→ [0.019, -0.176, 0.548, 0.087, ..., -0.341]
↑ close vectors, similar meaning

"Buy groceries"
→ [-0.412, 0.723, -0.088, 0.641, ..., 0.195]
↑ far away, different meaning

Similarity in semantic space

Nearby points capture related meaning and relationships.

Closeness is the key idea

  • Compare vectors by distance
  • Closer = more relevant
We do not search for matching words. We search for nearby meaning.
